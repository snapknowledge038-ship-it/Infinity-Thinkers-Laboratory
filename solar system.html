Solar System

What is the Solar System?

The Solar System is the gravitationally bound system of the Sun and the objects that orbit it. This includes 8 major planets, dwarf planets, moons, asteroids, and comets.

Inner Planets

Mercury The smallest planet and the closest to the Sun.
Venus The hottest planet in the Solar System.
Earth The only planet confirmed to host life.
Mars Appears red due to iron oxide on its surface.

Outer Planets

Jupiter The largest planet in the Solar System.
Saturn Known for its spectacular and extensive ring system.
Uranus A gas giant that rotates on a highly tilted axis.
Neptune The most distant and extremely cold planet from the Sun.

Dwarf Planets

While Pluto was once considered the 9th planet, it was reclassified as a 'Dwarf Planet' in 2006. Eris and Ceres are other notable dwarf planets in our system.
