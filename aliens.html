ALIENS

Beyond Our World

Are we truly alone? The term "Extraterrestrial Life" encompasses everything from microbial life on distant moons to highly advanced star-faring civilizations.

The Grey Visitors

  • Observation: Often described as 4-foot tall beings with translucent grey skin and large, liquid-black eyes.
  • Technology: Witnesses claim they possess craft that defy gravity and can vanish in seconds.
  • Abduction Lore: Thousands claim to have been taken aboard their ships for mysterious biological research.

The Fermi Paradox

If the universe is infinite, where is everybody?

  • The Great Filter: Civilizations might destroy themselves before they can reach us.
  • Cosmic Silence: We might be looking for radio signals while they use something far more advanced.
  • Dark Forest Theory: Intelligent life stays hidden to avoid being destroyed by predators.
