CERBERUS

The Hound of Hades

Cerberus is the monstrous multi-headed dog that guards the gates of the Underworld, preventing the dead from leaving and the living from entering without permission.

Physical Description

  • Three Heads: Most legends describe him with three heads, representing the past, present, and future.
  • Serpent's Tail: Instead of a normal tail, Cerberus often had a snake for a tail or snakes sprouting from his body.
  • Deadly Saliva: It is said that where his saliva fell to the ground, poisonous plants like aconite would grow.

The Final Labor

Slaying or capturing Cerberus was the 12th and final labor of Hercules. Hercules had to wrestle the beast using only his bare hands to prove his strength to King Eurystheus.
