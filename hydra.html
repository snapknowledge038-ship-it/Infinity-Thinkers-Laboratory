THE HYDRA

The Serpent of Lerna

In Greek mythology, the Lernaean Hydra was a terrifying multi-headed water serpent that guarded the entrance to the Underworld. It was known for its regenerative powers and deadly venom.

Legendary Abilities

  • Head Regeneration: For every one head that was cut off, two more would grow back in its place.
  • Toxic Breath: Its breath was so poisonous that it could kill a man instantly, and even its tracks were lethal.
  • Immortal Head: Among its many heads, one was said to be completely immortal, making the beast nearly impossible to kill.

The Second Labor of Hercules

The hero Hercules was tasked with slaying the beast. He eventually succeeded by using fire to cauterize the stumps of the necks after cutting off each head, preventing them from growing back.
